What I Do

I make wearable ornament especially with metal. Sterling silver, gold, and brass are my favourite. Then use hammers or acid etching for patterns, or a fine saw to cut out organic or geometric shapes. I like pieces that have traces of handmade, not perfection. Just like kid's drawing, which makes it only one in the entire universe.

Who I Am

Born and raised in Japan. Now I reside in Aotearoa. Studied metalsmithing at Savanna College of Art and Design in Georgia, USA.

In my spare time, I love walking with my dog, Ollie, looking for unique stones and beads online in a massage chair, and making pressed flowers in my sketching book.
